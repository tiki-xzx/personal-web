Birds View Mosaic

2018 Fall | Individual Work | Instructor: Leena Cho

A path is where your body is guided to move along, but your sight wonâ€™t be limited inside. It might be some distant attraction that gives you the overall direction, but it may not be seen all the time due to the change of topography, vegetation or some other factors. With these variations, by every step, you can have a different scene. Your view can be really limited when surrounded by dense trees, while it can also get quite far if there are only short trees, shrubs or even nothing in your sight. Contrast of different view depth and view shed can bring people different feeling. While creating a new forest mosaic for different view, different habitats for animals, such as birds, can also be created. They will be attracted by the better living condition, and themselves will be an attraction to people in the forest.
